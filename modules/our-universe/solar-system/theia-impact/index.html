NASA says the Moon may have started with a giant crash. A big world hit young Earth and blasted hot rock into space.

Theia is the name scientists often use for the huge object that may have crashed into Earth. It could have been about the size of Mars.

The crash threw melted and vaporized rock into space. That material gathered together and became the Moon.
